WHO AM I?

My name is Eric Jiang. I am a creator through and throgh. Throughout every phase of my life, very few things have remained constant; and one of those is my desire to make "stuff". Stuff includes (but is not limited to) miniramps for my fingerboards, a slew of logos and posters (of varying degrees of quality), apps, photos and video. For better or worse, this need to create has never waned and I have set out to turn this addiction into a career.
